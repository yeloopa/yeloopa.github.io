PhD research interests

My PhD thesis focuses on methodological and statistical issues in psychology using Parapsychology as a case study. In particular, I'm looking at the psi ganzfeld experiment and how researcher degrees of freedom have influenced the conclusions made in the parapsychology literature.

Other research interests

Outside of my PhD topic I have a background in health statistics and I am currently contributing to a meta-analysis assessing the use of stepped-wedge diagrams in medical intervention studies. Alongside this I am also leading a meta-analysis examing the longitudinal outcomes of school suspension.
